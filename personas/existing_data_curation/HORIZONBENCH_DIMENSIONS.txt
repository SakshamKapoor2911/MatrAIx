Dataset Details
===============
Paper: HorizonBench: Long-Horizon Personalization with Evolving Preferences (Li et al., 2026)
arXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.17283
HF Dataset: https://huggingface.co/datasets/stellalisy/HorizonBench
Config: mental_state_graphs
Size: 360 user timelines
Interactions: 1.6M turns over 6 months
Models: Claude-Sonnet-4.5, o3, Gemini-3-flash
License: CC-BY-4.0

Each user has:
- Demographics: age, gender, ethnicity, location, occupation, education, interests
- 6-month timeline with evolving preferences across all 30 domains
- ~4,300 conversation turns
- ~163K tokens per user
- Preference changes tracked with timestamps

Manifest: personas/existing_data_curation/manifests/horizonbench_mental_state_graphs.json
Added to dimensions+new.json: 2026-06-20
Dimension IDs in MatrAIx: horizonbench_dimension_1 through horizonbench_dimension_30
