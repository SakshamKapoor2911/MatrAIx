# Nemotron Domain User Characteristics

This summary describes the main characteristics of the selected Nemotron users in each application domain. It is based on the 50 selected users per domain and the within-domain cluster summaries.

## Movie / film

- A mix of direct media practitioners and media-adjacent users: actors, producers/directors, camera/editing operators, entertainment workers, and documentary/interview profiles.
- Includes creator profiles focused on acting, filmmaking, documentary work, animation, theater, and independent/streaming content.
- Also includes broader users whose arts, theater, photography, music, education, or community-storytelling interests make them useful for movie/film application tests.

## Beauty

- Strong professional coverage of hair, salon, cosmetology, skincare, nail care, barbering, massage, wellness, and fashion-related personas.
- Includes both service-provider personas and style/beauty consumers, which is useful for testing recommendations, personalization, and product/service discovery.
- Clusters separate hands-on salon/skincare workers, nail-focused personas, fashion/style personas, and broader wellness/personal-care profiles.

## Game

- Contains several distinct game-relevant groups: younger hobby gamers, strategy/competitive players, arcade/vending service workers, casino/gambling workers, and creative/game-art personas.
- The domain is less occupation-diverse than the others because many highly relevant profiles concentrate in gaming, amusement, casino, or student/hobbyist categories.
- Useful for testing game recommendation, player segmentation, safety/risk, casino/gambling-adjacent behavior, and game-creation applications.

## Finance

- Dominated by practical finance professionals: financial managers, advisors, investment analysts, accountants/auditors, tax preparers, bookkeeping clerks, and budget analysts.
- Clusters separate tax/bilingual finance service profiles, investment/management/advisory profiles, and accounting/audit/bookkeeping profiles.
- Good fit for testing financial advice personalization, budgeting, planning, compliance-sensitive recommendations, and professional/consumer finance workflows.

## Medical / healthcare

- Covers clinical, administrative, support, and therapy-oriented healthcare users: nurses, physicians, medical assistants, health service managers, medical records specialists, personal care aides, therapists, and healthcare support workers.
- Clusters distinguish patient-care professionals, medical administration/records, therapy and mental-health-adjacent profiles, bilingual/community healthcare profiles, and physician/assistant groups.
- Useful for testing healthcare communication, care-navigation, patient-support, clinical-adjacent workflow, and health-service personalization applications.

## Notes

- These are qualitative summaries for presentation and application-test planning, not claims about demographic balance.
- Cluster numbers are local within each domain and should not be compared across domains.
- The source personas are Nemotron YAML files only; Amazon-derived personas/data are not used.
