# Debt Consolidation

How people combine multiple debts into a single loan or program, why they do it, which providers they use, and what results they see.

## About this survey

This survey walks respondents from screening (current debt load, types of debt, and whether they have consolidated) through the specifics of their choice, including methods, providers such as SoFi, Marcus by Goldman Sachs, and National Debt Relief, loan amounts, interest rates, and repayment terms. It measures attitudes with agreement and importance matrix batteries, tracks behaviors such as closing cards and paying more than the minimum, and captures outcomes through satisfaction ratings, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, credit-score change, and confidence in becoming debt-free. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, varied Likert scales, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text for amounts and payments. It closes with a comprehensive demographic block covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, homeownership, and credit-score range.
