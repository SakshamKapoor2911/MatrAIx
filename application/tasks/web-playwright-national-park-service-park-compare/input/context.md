# Scenario Context: National Park Service

The National Park Service (NPS) preserves un-impaired natural and cultural resources of the National Park System.

Key details:
- Base URL: `https://www.nps.gov/`
- Park pages: `/yell/` (Yellowstone), `/yose/` (Yosemite), `/grca/` (Grand Canyon), `/zion/` (Zion).
- Information axes: Things to do, Basic Information, Alerts & Conditions, Fees & Passes.
