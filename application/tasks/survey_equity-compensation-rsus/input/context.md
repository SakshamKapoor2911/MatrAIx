# Equity Compensation and RSUs

How employees receive and manage restricted stock units and stock options, including vesting, sell strategy, and their understanding of the mechanics and taxes.

## About this survey

This survey screens for the type of equity compensation respondents hold (RSUs, ISOs, NSOs, PSUs, and more) and whether their company is public or private, then explores vesting schedules, grant value, exercise and sell-to-cover strategy, 10b5-1 plans, tax handling, and the platforms (Carta, Morgan Stanley at Work, Fidelity, and others) that manage the equity. It gauges attitudes through agreement and importance matrix batteries and probes advanced topics such as diversification, 83(b) elections, and AMT risk. Outcomes and intent are measured with satisfaction, realized value, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, and negotiation likelihood. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, varied Likert scales, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text for grant and realized values. It ends with demographics covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, industry, role level, and company stage.
