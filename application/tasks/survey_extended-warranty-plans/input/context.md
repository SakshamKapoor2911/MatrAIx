# Extended Warranty and Protection Plan

How consumers decide to buy extended warranties and protection plans, what they cover, and whether owners feel they are worth it.

## About this survey

This survey screens for extended warranty purchase behavior and the product categories covered, then captures the retailer or provider, plan brand, most expensive item covered, plan cost, coverage term, payment method, covered perils, and how the plan was presented. It measures decision drivers through an importance battery (price, accidental damage, term, reputation, claims ease, repair cost, failure-proneness) and attitudes through an agreement battery on worth, understanding, sales pressure, and manufacturer responsibility, plus a top-3 ranking of reasons and a purchase-frequency item. Drill-down sections cover claims history, satisfaction, savings, and coverage completeness, followed by overall worth, satisfaction, future intent, general stance, and a 0-10 likelihood-to-recommend scale. Numeric free-text captures plan cost and largest claim savings, with an optional open comment. A comprehensive demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, devices owned, and homeownership.
