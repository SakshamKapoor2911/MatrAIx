# Crypto Staking

How cryptocurrency holders stake assets to earn yield, including the platforms, rewards, risks, and lockup periods involved.

## About this survey

This survey screens for staking experience and the assets staked (ETH, SOL, ADA, and others), then examines the platforms and methods used, from Coinbase and Kraken to Lido, Rocket Pool, and self-run validators, along with custody choices, APY, liquid staking, and lockup understanding. It measures attitudes with agreement and importance matrix batteries and probes risk topics including slashing losses, regulation concerns, tax handling, and validator commissions. Outcomes and intent are captured through satisfaction, rewards earned, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, and future-staking likelihood. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, varied Likert scales, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text for staked value and rewards. The survey concludes with demographics covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, crypto experience, risk tolerance, and portfolio allocation.
