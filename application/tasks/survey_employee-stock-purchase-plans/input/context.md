# Employee Stock Purchase Plan

How employees use workplace stock purchase plans, including the discount, contribution rate, and the decision to hold or sell shares.

## About this survey

This survey screens for ESPP eligibility and participation, then drills into plan specifics such as the discount percentage, lookback provision, contribution rate, offering period, and which administrator (Fidelity, Morgan Stanley at Work, Schwab, Computershare, and others) holds the shares. It measures the hold-versus-sell decision and its drivers, concentration risk, and tax understanding, and captures attitudes through agreement and importance matrix batteries. Outcomes are assessed with satisfaction, realized gains, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, and future-contribution intent. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, varied Likert scales, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text for contributions and gains. It closes with demographics covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, industry, role level, and tenure.
