# Umbrella Insurance

Awareness, ownership, and attitudes toward personal umbrella liability insurance and the assets and risks it is meant to protect.

## About this survey

This survey screens for familiarity with and ownership of personal umbrella liability insurance and the underlying home and auto policies, then captures the provider, whether it is bundled, coverage limit, annual cost, required underlying limits, and the assets and risk factors it protects. It measures decision drivers through an importance battery (premium, limit, bundling, reputation, legal defense, ease, rental-property risk) and attitudes through an agreement battery on value, understanding, adequacy of standard limits, and perceptions of who needs it, plus a top-3 ranking and standalone likelihood, importance, and frequency items on lawsuit risk, asset protection, and coverage reviews. Later sections cover satisfaction, purchase and retention intent, reasons for not carrying it, purchase triggers, and a 0-10 likelihood-to-recommend scale. Numeric free-text captures annual premium and number of vehicles, with an optional open comment. A comprehensive demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, homeownership, and net worth.
