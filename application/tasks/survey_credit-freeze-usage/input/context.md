# Credit Freeze Usage

How consumers freeze or lock their credit at the major bureaus, why they do it, and how the process works for them.

## About this survey

This survey covers respondents' experience with credit freezes and locks at Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion, beginning with familiarity, current status, and what prompted them to act. It records behaviors such as how freezes were placed, which monitoring products they use, how often and why they thaw, and how long thawing takes. Attitudes are captured through agreement and importance matrix batteries, and outcomes through satisfaction ratings, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, and confidence in their identity protection. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, varied Likert scales, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text for costs and thaw counts. The survey ends with demographics covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, homeownership, technology comfort, and near-term credit plans.
