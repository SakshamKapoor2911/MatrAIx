# Balance Transfer Credit Card

How people use balance-transfer credit cards and 0% APR offers to consolidate and pay down credit card debt, and the outcomes they experience.

## About this survey

This survey screens for balance transfer behavior and current credit card debt, then captures the card used, the 0% intro period, transfer fee, amount transferred, how the card was found, feature priorities, limit utilization, and post-transfer spending. It measures decision drivers through an importance battery (intro period, transfer fee, annual fee, credit limit, ongoing APR, rewards) and attitudes through an agreement battery on debt payoff, understanding, planning, and temptation, plus a top-3 ranking and a payment-frequency item. Drill-down sections cover debt-payoff strategies, tracking tools, payoff progress, treatment of old cards, debt change, and score change, followed by satisfaction, reuse intent, current debt situation, and a 0-10 likelihood-to-recommend scale. Numeric free-text captures total debt, amount transferred, and estimated interest saved, with an optional open comment. A comprehensive demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, number of credit cards, and budgeting habits.
