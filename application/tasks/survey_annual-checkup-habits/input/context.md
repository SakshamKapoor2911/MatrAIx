# Annual Checkup Habits

Consumer habits and attitudes around the annual physical or wellness checkup, from scheduling and preparation to follow-up.

## About this survey

This survey measures whether respondents have a primary care provider, how recently and how often they get checkups, and how they schedule and prepare for them. It captures what happens during a typical visit, Likert batteries on the checkup experience (feeling rushed, understanding, provider communication) and the importance of factors such as provider continuity, wait time, and clear results, plus reasons for keeping up with or skipping checkups. It also covers follow-through on recommendations, how results are received, satisfaction and quality ratings, a likelihood-to-recommend scale, and a top-3 ranking of improvements. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, several Likert scales, a matrix battery split per item, numeric free-text items, and a demographic block covering age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, area type, insurance, and self-rated health.
