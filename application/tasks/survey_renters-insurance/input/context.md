# Renters Insurance

How renters obtain, understand, and evaluate renters insurance, and what drives their coverage, provider, and claims decisions.

## About this survey

This survey screens for housing situation and renters insurance status, then captures the provider, premium, tenure, how the policy was purchased, personal property and liability limits, deductible, replacement-cost basis, optional endorsements, and bundling. It measures decision drivers through an importance battery (price, coverage, liability, claims, reputation, digital experience) and attitudes through an agreement battery on value, understanding, and trust, plus a top-3 ranking of choice factors and a frequency item on belongings inventories. Drill-down sections cover claims history, handling, payout, and resolution time, followed by satisfaction, premium changes, renewal and switching intent, and a 0-10 likelihood-to-recommend scale. Numeric free-text captures monthly premium, years renting, and claim payout, with an optional open comment. A comprehensive demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, rental type, and roommate status.
