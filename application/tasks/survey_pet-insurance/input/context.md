# Pet Insurance

How pet owners choose, use, and evaluate pet health insurance, including plan design, cost, claims, and provider satisfaction.

## About this survey

This survey screens for pet ownership and pet insurance status, then captures the provider, coverage type, monthly premium, reimbursement level, deductible, annual payout limit, optional coverages, and the age of the insured pet. It measures decision drivers through an importance battery (premium, reimbursement, deductible, payout, condition coverage, claim speed, age limits) and attitudes through an agreement battery on value, understanding, and trust, plus a top-3 ranking of plan factors and a claim-frequency item. Drill-down sections cover veterinary spending, claims history, reimbursement satisfaction, and payout timing, followed by satisfaction, premium changes, renewal intent, and a 0-10 likelihood-to-recommend scale. Numeric free-text captures number of pets, monthly premium, annual vet spending, and largest claim, with an optional open comment. A comprehensive demographics block records age, gender, race/ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, pet types owned, and homeownership.
