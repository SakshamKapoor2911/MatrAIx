# Gift Card Usage

How people buy, use, give, manage, and resell gift cards.

## About this survey

This survey profiles consumer behavior and attitudes around gift cards, beginning with a screener on how often respondents buy or receive them and who they buy for. It records the card brands and retailers involved such as Amazon, Target, Starbucks, and open-loop Visa cards, where cards are purchased, gifting occasions, physical versus digital preference, and experience with resale services like CardCash and Raise. Attitudes are measured with an agreement Likert battery on thoughtfulness, forgetfulness, overspending, and fraud concern, plus a per-item importance matrix over gift-card selection factors, a top-three ranking of reasons for liking gift cards, and multi-selects of frustrations and reasons for gifting. Additional sections cover unused balances, balance-checking, scam exposure, organization, and outcomes including satisfaction, recommendation likelihood, and future purchase and resale intent. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, varied Likert scales, an importance matrix, a ranking item, and numeric free-text. The survey concludes with a demographic block covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, household income, education, employment, household size, marital status, U.S. region, and urbanicity.
