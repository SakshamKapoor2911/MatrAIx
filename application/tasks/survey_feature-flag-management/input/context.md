# Feature Flag Management

How software teams adopt, manage, and feel about feature flags and toggles.

## About this survey

This survey profiles how teams use feature flags, opening with a screener on frequency, tenure, role, and primary tool. It records the flag tools used, what flags are used for, SDK languages, deployment model, who can toggle flags, cleanup handling, targeting rules, CI/CD integration, and experimentation use. Attitudes are captured with an agreement Likert battery on safer shipping, flag debt, testability, and release speed, and a per-item importance matrix over what matters when choosing a platform, plus frequency scales for kill-switch use and problems from stale flags. Additional sections cover flag lifecycle policy, rollout strategy, who uses flags, evaluation location, a top-three ranking of reasons to use flags, attitudes about flag longevity, satisfaction with the tool and governance, recommendation likelihood, and tool-change intent. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, Likert scales, an importance matrix, a ranking item, and numeric free-text. The survey concludes with a demographic block covering role, professional experience, employment, company size, industry, age, gender, race and ethnicity, education, U.S. region, and urbanicity.
