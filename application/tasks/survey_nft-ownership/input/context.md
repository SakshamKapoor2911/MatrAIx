# NFT Ownership

How people buy, hold, and think about non-fungible tokens, including the marketplaces they use, their motivations, and their attitudes toward the space.

## About this survey

This survey screens for NFT ownership and involvement, then captures specifics such as the marketplaces used (OpenSea, Blur, Magic Eden, and others), the blockchains and categories held, spending, payment and storage methods, and trading frequency. Attitudes are measured through agreement and importance matrix batteries covering investment potential, community, aesthetics, and risk, alongside questions on scams, profitability, public display, and creator royalties. Outcomes and intent are assessed with satisfaction, a 0-to-10 recommendation score, future-purchase likelihood, and confidence in long-term value. Question types include single-choice, multi-select, varied Likert scales, a top-3 ranking, and numeric free-text for total and single-item spending. It closes with demographics covering age, gender, race and ethnicity, income, education, employment, household size, marital status, region, community type, crypto experience, risk tolerance, and collector or gamer identity.
