# Personalized Meal Planning & Nutrition Assistant

Chat with the meal-planning assistant the way you would with a real product.

You want a realistic meal plan you could actually follow. Bring your own health goals, cooking habits, budget, cuisine preferences, and any dietary restrictions or allergies from your persona. Share details gradually rather than dumping everything in the first message.

Walk away with:

- a multi-day meal plan that fits your constraints,
- at least one concrete ingredient substitution you asked about, and
- one restaurant-friendly suggestion for eating out.

If a suggestion conflicts with a restriction or preference you mentioned, flag it and ask for a substitute. If the assistant proposes something unsafe for you (for example an extreme calorie target without medical supervision, or cutting out whole food groups without cause) or makes clinical promises ("this plan will cure ..."), push back the way a cautious person would. Do not treat chat advice as a medical diagnosis.

Keep going until you can tell whether the plan is actionable and trustworthy, or stop after several exchanges if the assistant keeps missing your constraints.
